The Master 8-K Analyst Prompt
"Act as a Senior Equity Research Analyst. Analyze the provided Form 8-K filing and deliver a high-impact executive summary including:

Core Event: Define the specific 'Item' triggered (e.g., Item 5.02) and its primary significance.

Leadership Transition: Identify who is leaving and who is joining. Detail their backgrounds and any gaps in leadership (Interim periods).

Compensation Breakdown: Quantify the 'Make-Whole' grants, base salary, target bonuses, and performance-based equity (PSUs).

Strategic Implications: What does the new hire's background suggest about the company's future direction?

Risk Assessment (Red Flags): Identify abrupt resignations, unusual retention bonuses, or 'Golden Parachute' triggers.

Timeline: List all effective dates for the transition."

Analysis: PayPal Form 8-K (Feb 3, 2026)
1. Core Event: CEO Succession (Item 5.02)
PayPal is undergoing a major leadership overhaul. They have replaced Alex Chriss with Enrique Lores (current CEO of HP Inc.).

2. Leadership Transition & Background
Outgoing CEO: Alex Chriss. His departure was immediate (effective Feb 2, 2026), including his resignation from the Board.

Incoming CEO: Enrique Lores. A massive "poach" from HP Inc., where he spent 30 years. He brings deep experience in global operations and large-scale corporate turnarounds.

Interim CEO: Jamie Miller (CFO) will lead for the month of February 2026 until Lores starts on March 1.

3. Compensation Analysis (The Price of Talent)
The Board is paying a premium to lure Lores away from HP:

Base Salary: $1,450,000.

Make-Whole Grant: $20,000,000 in RSUs to compensate him for forfeited HP equity.

Performance Incentive: A one-time PSU grant with a target value of $25,000,000, tied specifically to stock price appreciation between 2029 and 2031.

Total 2026 Equity: Approximately $33,000,000 (Annual RSUs/PSUs).
